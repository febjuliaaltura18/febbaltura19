In today’s digital world, faith and integrity should guide everything we do online. “Una ang Ginoo” reminds me to put God first in my decisions, words, and actions—even in the digital space.

Educational Page: Digital Ethics

Digital ethics teaches us how to behave responsibly online. This includes being honest in our work, avoiding plagiarism, respecting privacy, and practicing online kindness.

Ethical digital practices help create a safe and respectful online environment where everyone feels valued.
